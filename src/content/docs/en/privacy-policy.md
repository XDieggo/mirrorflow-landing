# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 10, 2026

MirrorFlow respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect and use your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Purchase Information
- Name and email (for license delivery)
- Payment information processed by MercadoPago (we do not store card data)

### 1.2 Technical Information
- Installation data for license activation
- Anonymous error logs (optional, for product improvement)

### 1.3 Communications
- WhatsApp messages for technical support

## 2. Use of Information

We use your information to:
- Deliver and activate your software license
- Provide technical support
- Send product updates
- Improve the software

## 3. Data Sharing

**We do not sell or share your personal information** with third parties, except:
- MercadoPago for payment processing
- When required by law

## 4. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:
- Encrypted communications
- Restricted access to personal data
- Secure storage

## 5. Cookies

Our website may use technical cookies to:
- Remember language and theme preferences
- Anonymous traffic analytics

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request correction or deletion of data
- Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp.

## 7. Data Retention

We maintain your license information while the product is active. Support messages are kept for 1 year.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify significant changes by email.
